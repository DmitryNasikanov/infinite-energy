Mirror Electrochromics

TiO‚ÇÇ is used to control mirror orientation in space:

Component Function
TiO‚ÇÇ layer Changes reflectivity when voltage is applied
Effect Uneven light pressure ‚Üí torque
Control Mirror tilting without engines or fuel

Principle: A 100√ó100 m mirror with electrochromic coating. When voltage is applied to one edge, reflectivity decreases ‚Üí light pressure becomes uneven ‚Üí the mirror slowly rotates. This allows directing reflected light to the receiver without fuel consumption.

Electrochromic layer mass: ~1 kg/mirror (out of 116 kg total mass).

Advantages of Electrochromic Control

  • No mechanical parts
  • Powered by solar panels
  • Service life = mirror service life
