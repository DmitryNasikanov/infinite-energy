American projects are inflated due to:

  1. Cost-plus contracts ‚Äî contractor benefits from spending more
  2. Bureaucracy ‚Äî approvals, reviews, reports
  3. Contractor monopoly ‚Äî Boeing, Lockheed without competition
  4. Legal costs ‚Äî lawsuits, patents, insurance

Why Helios is different:

  1. International competition ‚Äî China, Russia, India are cheaper
  2. Mass production ‚Äî 1650 factories, 60,000 robots, economies of scale
  3. Fixed-price contracts ‚Äî not cost-plus
  4. Common goal ‚Äî fewer political games
