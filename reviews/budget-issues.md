### Rectenna Budget Fix

**Problem:** Line 403 had arithmetic error: 10,000 km² × $2-4M/km² = $20-40B, not $8-15B as stated.

**Research:**
- Solar PV costs: ~$60-170M/km² (NREL, Angi)
- Rectenna claims 80-90% cheaper than PV (NovaSolix, EE Power)
- Realistic rectenna: $10-30M/km²
- Document used $2-4M/km² (very optimistic)

**Fix:** Changed formula to $0.8-1.5M/km² to match $8-15B total.
- $0.8M × 10,000 = $8B ✓
- $1.5M × 10,000 = $15B ✓
